Getting Here

McMenamins Edgefield is located at 2126 SW Halsey St, Troutdale, OR — about 30 minutes east of downtown Portland. Portland International Airport (PDX) is the nearest major airport, roughly 15 minutes away.

Staying On-Site

Edgefield has its own hotel on the property — a beautiful historic manor. We highly recommend booking a room to make a full weekend of it. We have reserved a room block; booking details and rate code will be shared soon.

Parking

Complimentary parking is available on-site at Edgefield. Additional details will be posted here as we finalise logistics.

Things To Do

★ ✦ ☀ ✦ ★

Edgefield Grounds

Wander the 74-acre estate — visit the winery, brewery, distillery, soaking pool, and cinema. There is no shortage of ways to spend a sunny Oregon afternoon on property.

Explore the Columbia River Gorge

Just minutes away, the Columbia River Gorge offers breathtaking hikes, waterfalls, and scenic overlooks. Crown Point and Multnomah Falls are not to be missed.

Explore Portland

Portland is a city of forests, food, and peculiar charm. Wander Powell's Books, stroll the Japanese Garden, or lose an afternoon in the Pearl District.

FAQs

What is the dress code?

Formal or semi-formal attire. We welcome soft, romantic tones — blush, peach, navy, and ivory are all very much at home here.

Are children welcome?

We love your little ones, but the ceremony and reception are adults-only events. We hope you'll treat the evening as a rare night out.

Will there be parking?

Yes — complimentary parking is available on-site at McMenamins Edgefield.

Can I stay at Edgefield?

Absolutely, and we encourage it! The historic hotel on property is a wonderful experience. Watch for our room block details in your formal invitation.
