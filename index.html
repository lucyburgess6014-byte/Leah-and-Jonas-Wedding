Travel

Getting Here

McMenamins Edgefield is located at 2126 SW Halsey St, Troutdale, OR — about 30 minutes east of downtown Portland. Portland International Airport (PDX) is the nearest major airport, roughly 15 minutes away.

Staying On-Site

We have reserved a room block with limited space on the premises. To reserve a room, please call the front desk at (503) 669-8610 and tell them "This is for the wedding of Leah Batterson" when making the reservation.

There will be rooms with a king bed option and a queen bed option. Please be aware these are individual bedrooms, but private bathrooms are available in the hallway.

Additional Places to Stay

Troutdale: If you are wanting to stay near the venue, one of the closest hotels is Best Western.

Portland: If you are wanting to stay near Multnomah Village where Leah and Jonas, and Jonas's parents live, the closest motel is Capitol Hill Motel.

Parking

Complimentary parking is available on-site at Edgefield. Please drive responsibly!

Things To Do

★ ✦ ☀ ✦ ★

On the Edgefield Grounds

McMenamins Edgefield has a lot of fun activities on the premises — feel free to show up early and enjoy at your own leisure! Things to enjoy include:

  • Soaking Pool
  • Golf Course
  • Theater
  • Lots of Restaurants and Bars

Explore the Columbia River Gorge

Just minutes away, the Columbia River Gorge offers breathtaking hikes, waterfalls, and scenic overlooks. Crown Point and Multnomah Falls are not to be missed.

Explore Portland

The Portland area has many fun amenities to see and do. Wander Powell's Books, stroll the Japanese Garden, lose an afternoon in the Pearl District — and make sure to get a delicious Voodoo Doughnut!
